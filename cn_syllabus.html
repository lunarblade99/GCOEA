CSU603 COMPUTER NETWORK 


Teaching Scheme:03L+00T                     Total:03                   Credits:03
Evaluation Scheme:15 CT1 + 15 CT2 +10 TA +60 ESE                  Total Marks:100
Duration of ESE :2 hrs 30 min

Topics:

  1. Overview of OSI reference model: Topology design, Media Access Control Level, Services, Problems and protocols.

  2. Practical local area network design and implementation: IEEE LAN Standards, Logical Link Control protocols, HDLC, ALOHA, SLOTTED ALOHA, FDDI, Client Server model and related software’s.

  3. Network Layer: Level services, problems and protocols. WAN, MAN, interconnection networks related software’s. TCP/IP, Novel NetWare, Routers, Bridges and Gateways. X.25, Internet and related software’s NETSCAPE and MOSAIC.

  4. Transport layer: services, problems and their protocol. Brief functioning of upper layers, E-mail and other application



TextBook:
  1. Computer Networking: A top down Approach Featuring the internet, Jim Kurose ,Keith Ross,2nd edition, Addison Wesley,July2002.
